## Full Description (Max 4000 characters)
**Aarogyam AI: Empowering Rural Health with Intelligence**

Aarogyam AI is a specialized healthcare assistant designed specifically for rural and low-resource environments in India. Built to work where traditional healthcare is far and internet connectivity is sparse, Aarogyam AI provides immediate guidance, screening, and life-saving information in your own language.

### Key Features:

*   **Symptom Analysis (English, Hindi, Marathi):** Speak or type your symptoms in your local language. Our AI, trained on rural health patterns, helps identify possible conditions like Malaria, Dengue, Typhoid, and more.
*   **Skin Condition Detection:** Upload a photo of a rash, wound, or skin infection for instant AI-powered visual analysis.
*   **Offline First Aid Encyclopedia:** Access critical guides for snakebites, sunstroke, dog bites, electric shocks, and burns—even without an internet connection.
*   **Smart Assessment Reports:** Receive detailed reports explaining the "Why" behind findings, along with clear "Next Steps" for recovery.
*   **Health History Log:** Keep a local record of your past assessments to show to a doctor or healthcare worker during your next visit.
*   **Emergency SOS:** One-tap access to emergency services (Ambulance 102, Police 100).
*   **Nearby Hospital Finder:** Locate the nearest medical facilities and government hospitals using GPS (requires internet).

### Why Aarogyam AI?
In rural India, the first few hours of a health crisis are the most critical. Aarogyam AI provides the bridge between feeling unwell and getting professional medical help. It is designed to be culturally sensitive, linguistically accurate, and high-performance on all mobile devices.

### Important Disclaimer:
Aarogyam AI is an **informational tool only**. It does NOT provide final medical diagnoses. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional or visit a clinic for any medical concerns. In case of emergencies like chest pain or severe difficulty breathing, call 102 immediately.
